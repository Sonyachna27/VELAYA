OUR STORY

Velaya was born from personal health journeys that led us — Lena and Yulia — to rethink everything we put on our bodies. After facing serious wellness challenges, we realized that even hair care matters — because hair absorbs what we feed it.

We created Velaya to be different: clean, effective, and safe. Made in Italy, designed for Velaya USA, our formulas use only pure ingredients that respect both your health and your beauty.

Velaya is more than hair care — it’s a daily ritual of self-respect.

Our values

  • Gentleness - in everything from formulas to presentation
  • Balance - between efficacy and delicacy
  • Everyday - products that don't require a special occasion
  • Minimalism - nothing superfluous, only what works.
